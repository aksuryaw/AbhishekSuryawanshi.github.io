Fairfax County
Real Estate Analysis

In this project, we perform an entire data analysis on Redfin's Fairfax County housing data. We clean, explore, and visualize the data. We curated the raw dataset to remove data redundancy, duplication, and to standardize the data format. An exploratory analysis was conducted to highlight key attributes and their relationships with each other. The dashboard gives an overview of the Fairfax County Housing market and its major players. The dashboard includes custom slicers that enable the user to filter by desired parameters to redefine the data for themselves.
